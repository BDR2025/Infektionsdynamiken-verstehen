What it’s about

Explore how outbreaks grow

Understanding Infection Dynamics helps you see how infections spread by letting you experiment with simple sliders and easy-to-read charts. In each mini-lab you can try what happens when people recover faster or protective measures are taken. Learning about infections becomes clear practical and even fun.

We make infection dynamics easy to understand interactive and engaging.

About the project

Open · Transparent · Scientific

Understanding Infection Dynamics is an Open Educational Resources project. All content is free to use with no sign-up and no cookies. The goal is accessible knowledge on infection dynamics that is independent and easy to understand.

The spark came from a simple browser tool which we keep available as CLASSIC.
