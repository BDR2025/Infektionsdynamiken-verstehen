Scientific vision

Understanding infection dynamics

Understanding Infection Dynamics presents epidemiological models with scientific rigour and didactic clarity. Modular mini-labs enable parameter control, simulation and direct interpretation through live metrics and curves. This yields transparent and reproducible understanding suited to academic study and practice.

About the project

Open · Transparent · Scientific

Understanding Infection Dynamics is an Open Educational Resources project. All content is free to use with no sign-up and no cookies. The goal is accessible knowledge on infection dynamics that is independent and easy to understand.

The spark came from a simple browser tool which we keep available as CLASSIC.
